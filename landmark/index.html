Famous Historical Landmark

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It was completed in 1889 and has become a global cultural icon of France. It stands 324 meters tall and was the tallest man-made structure in the world until the Chrysler Building was finished in 1930.

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications made of various materials, built to protect Chinese states and empires against invasions. It stretches over 13,000 miles and was constructed as early as the 7th century BC. Today, it is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a symbol of China's enduring strength.
